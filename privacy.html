Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026 · Effective date: June 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Best Bangkok Trading Co., Ltd. ("we", "us", "our") — the operator of the Doge & Orange pet-care brand — collects, uses, and shares information when you visit dogeorange.pages.dev, when you purchase our products through our official storefronts, and when our internal staff use our marketing tools that integrate with third-party platform APIs (including TikTok Marketing API).

By using this website, our products, or our internal tools, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you give us directly

When you purchase a Doge & Orange product through one of our official TikTok Shop or Shopee storefronts, the platform shares the following with us in order to fulfill your order:

  • Your shipping name and address
  • Your phone number (for delivery coordination only)
  • The product(s) you ordered and the order amount
  • Buyer messages you choose to send to the store

If you email us at wh8883408@gmail.com, we will see your email address and the content of your message.

2.2 Information we receive from third-party platforms (TikTok)

To monitor the performance of our own marketing activities, our internal team uses tools that connect to TikTok's official advertiser APIs (TikTok Marketing API) and TikTok Shop Open API. Through these APIs we receive data that belongs to our own brand accounts only — never personal data about other TikTok users. Specifically:

  • Our own ad campaign data: campaign names, ad-group structure, ad creative metadata (video ID, description, thumbnail URL), spend, impressions, click-through rate, cost per result, ROAS — but only for campaigns paid for by Best Bangkok Trading Co., Ltd.
  • Our own TikTok Shop sales data: SKUs, orders, GMV, fees, settlements, inventory levels — only for the storefronts we own and operate.
  • Affiliate creator performance for our own products: when an affiliate creator publicly chooses to promote our product, TikTok shares the resulting view count, sale count, and GMV attributed to that promotion. We do not receive personal data about the creator beyond their public TikTok handle.

We do not collect, ingest, or store any personal data about TikTok users who are not our direct customers.

2.3 Information collected automatically (this website)

When you browse this website, our hosting provider may log standard web request metadata (IP address, user agent, referring page, timestamp) for security and reliability purposes. We do not use third-party advertising trackers or behavioral profiling cookies on this website.

4. How long we keep data

  • Order data: 5 years from the order date (Thai tax-record requirement).
  • Marketing performance data from TikTok APIs: 24 months. After 24 months we keep only aggregated, non-identifiable summaries.
  • Email correspondence: 2 years from last reply, then deleted.
  • Website request logs: 90 days, then deleted.

5. Sharing & transfers

We share data only with the following categories of recipients, and only the minimum required for them to perform their function:

  • Logistics providers (J&T, Flash, Kerry, Thailand Post) — to deliver your order.
  • Selling platforms (TikTok Shop, Shopee) — they already have your order data because you placed the order through them; we do not transmit your data back to them.
  • Cloud infrastructure providers — for storing our internal business data securely. These providers process data on our behalf and do not have an independent right to use it.

Data may be processed in countries outside Thailand (e.g., in the case of cloud providers). Where this happens we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

6. Your rights

If you are a customer in Thailand, the PDPA (Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562) gives you the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Object to processing or withdraw consent
  • File a complaint with the Thailand PDPC

To exercise any of these rights, email wh8883408@gmail.com with the subject line "Privacy request". We will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We protect data using industry-standard practices: TLS encryption in transit, access controls on internal systems (only authorized Best Bangkok Trading employees can access TikTok API tokens and sales data), and prompt patching of known vulnerabilities. No system is perfectly secure, however; in the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal data we will notify you and the Thailand PDPC within 72 hours as required by law.

8. Children

Our products are sold to adults purchasing pet supplies. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top and, for material changes, post a notice on the homepage of this website for at least 30 days.
