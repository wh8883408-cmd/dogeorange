About the brand

Doge & Orange is a Thailand-based pet care brand operated by Best Bangkok Trading Co., Ltd., focused on practical, ingredient-conscious products for cats and small pets in Southeast Asia.

We started in 2025 with a single observation: pet owners in tropical Thailand spend more time cleaning food bowls and litter trays than almost any other daily pet-care task — and the existing options use either harsh detergents (unsafe if a cat licks the bowl) or low-absorbency clay litter (heavy, dusty, expensive to ship). We designed our first two products around that gap, and we grew the brand on TikTok Shop and Shopee Thailand by working directly with Thai pet creators.

All products are sold under the Doge & Orange brand across our official TikTok Shop and Shopee Thailand storefronts, and shipped from our Bangkok warehouse.
